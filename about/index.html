About Advent Hymnals

Preserving 160+ years of Adventist musical heritage through digital technology, making it accessible for worship, education, and research worldwide.

Our Mission

"Digitizing and archiving historical Adventist hymnals while making them accessible across multiple platforms and preserving their historical significance."

We believe that the rich musical heritage of the Seventh-day Adventist Church should be preserved and made accessible to current and future generations, supporting worship leaders, educators, researchers, and music enthusiasts worldwide.

Project Objectives

Preservation

Our Journey

2018

Project Launch

The Advent Hymnals project began with the vision to digitize and preserve historical Adventist hymnals.

2019-2021

Early Development

Versions 1-3 released with growing hymn collection and basic search functionality.

2022

Version 4 Release

Added improved search capabilities and enhanced user interface.

Dec 2023

Temporary Hiatus

Site went offline due to server costs, highlighting the need for sustainable funding.

2025

Version 5 & Revival

Partnership with Gospel Sounders enables project revival with focus on sustainability and accessibility.
