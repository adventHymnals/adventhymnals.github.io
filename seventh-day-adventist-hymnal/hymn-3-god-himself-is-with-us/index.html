#3God Himself Is With Us

Words: Gerhard Tersteegen
Music: Joachim Neander
Tune: Wunderbarer König
Meter: 6.6.8.3.3.6.6

Hymn Display

1
God Himself is with us; Let us all adore Him, And with awe appear before Him. God is here within us; Soul, in silence fear Him, Humbly, fervently draw near Him.
2
Come, abide within me; Let my soul, like Mary, Be Thine earthly sanctuary. Come and dwell within us; With transfiguring splendor, Soul, in silence be before Him.
3
Gladly we surrender Earth's deceitful treasures, Pride of life and sinful pleasures. Gladly, Lord, we offer Thine to be forever, Soul and life and each endeavor.
